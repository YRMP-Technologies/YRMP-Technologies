## About Us

**Yama Ramin Media Production and Technology Inc (YRMPT)** is a leading Media Production, Telecom, and IT corporation in Afghanistan with over **two decades of experience**. We have successfully completed **5,800+ projects** within scope, budget, and timeline, serving a diverse portfolio of national and international clients.

Our clients include the **Government of Afghanistan**, **United Nations**, **AWCC**, **Goethe-Institute**, **MTN**, **Roshan**, international organizations, private enterprises, and local institutions.

YRMPT is a trusted partner of major multinational telecom operators such as **Etisalat**, **MTN**, **Roshan**, and **Afghan Wireless**, delivering innovative mobile solutions and content under **ATRA NOC #9212**. Our strong focus on product development, quality assurance, and customer satisfaction is driven by deep expertise and long-standing experience in the telecom industry.
